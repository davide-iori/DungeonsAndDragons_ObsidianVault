Gargantuan Vehicle (Ship), No Alignment

Armor Class 15 (reinforced hull)
Hit Points 400 (damage threshold 15)
Speed 45 feet = 0.0085 knots (sailing)
Crew 30 (Captain, Helmsman, First Mate, 10 Gunners, 18 Crew)

## **Ship Weapons**

### Ballistae (4) (Long Range: 500 ft., Short Range: 250 ft.)
+6 to hit, range 120/480 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d10) piercing damage.


### Mangonels (2) (Long Range: 1,000 ft., Short Range: 500 ft.)
+5 to hit, range 200/800 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (5d10) bludgeoning damage.

### Swivel Guns (6) (Short Range: 250 ft.)
+7 to hit, range 100/300 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d8) piercing damage.


### Boarding Crew (20 Pirates, armed with cutlasses and crossbows)
When within 5 feet of another ship, the galleon can attempt to board, deploying up to 10 pirates per round.

## Actions (Ship Commands)

### Ram (Pilot Action):
The galleon can crash into another ship or creature, dealing damage based on the Crash Damage Table.

### Full Speed Ahead (Pilot Action):
The ship moves up to its maximum speed.

### Broadside Volley (Captain Action): 
Fire all available weapons on one side of the ship at a single target.

## Defenses

### Reinforced Hull:
Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks.

### Sails & Rigging: 
Targetable (AC 12, HP 100); if destroyed, ship speed is halved.

### Helm Suppression: 
If the spelljamming helm is disabled, the ship loses its flying capability and must rely on wind or drift.
