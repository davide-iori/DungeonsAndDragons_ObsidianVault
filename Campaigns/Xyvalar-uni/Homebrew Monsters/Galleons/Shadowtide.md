Shadowtide

Gargantuan Vehicle (Ship), No Alignment 

Armor Class 18 (ghostly hull, spectral reinforcements)
Hit Points 600 (damage threshold 20)
Speed 60 feet (sailing), 120 feet (submerged or spelljamming)
Crew Unknown (Rumored to be spectral undead)
___

Legendary Traits

Emergence of the Doomed: The Queenstorm can rise from beneath the waters or the Astral Sea without warning. It does not create waves, and no lookout sees it coming until it is within 500 feet.

No Survivors: Any creature reduced to 0 HP aboard Queenstorm automatically fails all death saves, their soul bound to the ship unless revived by a wish spell.

Ghostly Hull: The Queenstorm is resistant to all nonmagical damage and immune to psychic, necrotic, and poison damage.

Phantom Wind: Even in dead air, the Queenstorm moves at full speed. It cannot be stopped by natural weather effects.



---

Ship Weapons

Spectral Harpoons (6) (Long Range: 1,000 ft., Short Range: 500 ft.)

+9 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (4d10) necrotic and piercing damage. On a hit, the target must make a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be pulled 30 feet toward the ship.


Cursed Mangonels (2) (Long Range: 1,500 ft., Short Range: 750 ft.)

+8 to hit, range 250/1000 ft., one target. Hit: 36 (6d10) force damage. A creature hit must make a DC 18 Constitution save or be frightened for 1 minute.


Death’s Broadside (Recharge 5-6) (Fires all weapons in one round, but cannot fire next turn.)

The Drowning Deck (Melee Attack, Boarding Action)

Any creature stepping onto the Queenstorm’s deck must make a DC 17 Wisdom save or become frightened. At the start of their next turn, they must make a DC 15 Constitution save or begin drowning as ghostly water fills their lungs.




---

Legendary Actions (3 per round, used at the end of other turns)

Vanish Beneath the Waves (Costs 2 actions): The Queenstorm can submerge or rise instantly without using movement.

The Captain’s Curse: A spectral figure appears aboard an enemy ship, whispering a deathly prophecy to its captain. The target must make a DC 18 Charisma saving throw or be cursed, giving all attacks against them advantage for 1 minute.

Unholy Tempest (Costs 3 actions, Recharge 5-6): The Queenstorm summons a supernatural storm. All enemy ships within 1,000 feet must make a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be pushed 60 feet in a random direction. All nonmagical fires are extinguished, and ranged weapon attacks suffer disadvantage for 1 minute.



---

Defenses & Weaknesses

Hull of the Damned: The Queenstorm can only be permanently destroyed by a ship blessed by celestial magic or wielding an artifact of divine origin. Otherwise, it reforms within 1d10 nights.

Salt and Steel: Weapons made of blessed silver or enchanted with radiant energy deal double damage to the Queenstorm.



---

The Queenstorm is a ghostly terror of the seas and beyond, hunting the living without mercy. It is nearly unstoppable, appearing from the depths to annihilate all in its path. Some say it is the remnant of a lost pirate king’s vessel, forever cursed. Others believe it is Death’s own ship, ferrying souls who never reach the afterlife.

No one who has seen the Queenstorm and lived can recall how they escaped.
