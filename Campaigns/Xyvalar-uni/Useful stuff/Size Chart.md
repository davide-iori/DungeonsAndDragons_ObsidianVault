**In official capacities, a creature's size generally does not exceed the colossal level. Larger categories are for those who need to explore mathematical hypothetical situations where a creature exceeds the standard criteria. For those, refer to the [SRD Table of Creature Size and Scale article](https://dungeons.fandom.com/wiki/SRD:Table_of_Creature_Size_and_Scale)
